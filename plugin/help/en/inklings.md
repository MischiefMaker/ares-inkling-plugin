# Inklings
This command lets you explain your character's magic, special ability or superpower.

`inklings` - Shows you own gifts.

`inklings/set <title>=<details>` - Adds or edits a Gift with the title <title>.*
`inklings/edit <title>` - Pulls the named Gift into your client for editing.*
`inklings/delete <title>` - Deletes the named Gift from your Gifts.*


* These can only be set and edited during chargen.
